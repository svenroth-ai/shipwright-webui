The accepted `github-actions-mutable-action-tag` risk is registered in `.trivyignore.yaml` with an expiry, so the compliance dashboard flags it for re-review instead of the decision rotting unnoticed. To be explicit: this records an accepted risk accompanying a deliberate *un*pinning of GitHub-owned actions — it is not a hardening. Third-party actions remain SHA-pinned, which is where tag-repointing is an actual threat.
