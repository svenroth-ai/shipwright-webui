Hardened every scrollable dialog and lane against the panel-clipping bug fixed in v0.23.x: the rule that keeps a scrollable area from silently squeezing its contents shut now lives in one shared component (`ModalScrollBody`) instead of being re-typed per dialog, and a meta-test fails the build if any new scrollable area omits it. Two further spots that were one small style change away from the same bug — the Task Board column and the Campaigns lane — are now protected too. No visible change; this is purely a guard against the bug coming back somewhere else.
