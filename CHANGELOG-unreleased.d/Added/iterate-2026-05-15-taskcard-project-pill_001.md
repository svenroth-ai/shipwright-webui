TaskBoard cards now lead their meta row with a project-identity pill — the owning project's name beside a solid 8 px dot, both tinted and bordered in the project's accent color (the custom `settings.color` when set, else a deterministic hash-derived hue, via `getProjectColor`). A multi-project board is now scannable per-project at a glance; the faint 3 px left-edge strip alone was hard to read. The pill is read-only — clicking it navigates to TaskDetail like the rest of the card — and falls back to the raw `projectId` while the projects query is still loading. (iterate-2026-05-15-taskcard-project-pill, ADR-105)
