The Command Center now has a small, consistent motion system — the grade ring counts up, the mid-run proof lines and Ship's-Log entries slide in, and the artifact panel eases open — applied only where the movement means something (something arrived, is running, or a value is counting), never as decoration. Crucially, if your system is set to reduce motion (as Windows is when animations are turned off), every screen shows its complete, final state immediately: all content present and fully visible, counters showing their final number, the ring showing its full arc — nothing is ever hidden behind an animation. The embedded terminal is untouched.
