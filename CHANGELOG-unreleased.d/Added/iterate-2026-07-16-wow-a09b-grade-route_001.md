Intent wizard — the "Grade your repo" door is now live. Point it at a folder on your machine or a GitHub URL and it runs the real Control Grade read-only: nothing is written, no account is needed, and a URL is shallow-cloned to a temp folder and deleted right after. The card shows exactly what the grader found — including the honest "n/a" for anything it genuinely couldn't measure (the wizard never invents a score to fill a gap). If a repo can't be graded, the grade engine isn't installed, or the report comes back in a shape it doesn't recognise, you get a plain, honest message instead of a broken or fake card. "Adopt this repo →" hands the same folder straight to the adopt flow (FR-01.53).
