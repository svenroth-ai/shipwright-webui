The Mission tab's "Where it stands" now tracks the whole lifecycle with six stages — Analyze, Spec, Build, Test, Finalize, Merge — instead of four, so a session that has pushed and opened a PR is shown as "Merge" (awaiting the squash) rather than lumped into "Finalize". The stage is now read from the CURRENT piece of work: on a long autonomous campaign that runs many sub-iterates in one session, the tracker no longer freezes on "Merge" the moment the first sub-iterate opens its PR — it correctly shows the sub-iterate that is running right now. When the open session is a campaign, the panel also shows a progress line ("Sub-iterate 21 of 22 · A21") above the stage tracker and names the campaign in plain language. Everything stays honest: the stage still shows "—" when it cannot be derived, progress comes only from the real campaign data, and the tab remains a read-only observer that writes nothing.
