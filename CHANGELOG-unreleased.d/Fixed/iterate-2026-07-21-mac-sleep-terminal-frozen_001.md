Embedded terminal: recover on its own after the machine sleeps. The reconnect schedule used to give up after 5 attempts in ~6 seconds — too early for a VPN/Tailscale link that is still coming back after a resume — and then stopped trying entirely, leaving the terminal frozen (and unscrollable, since scrolling is sent over the same connection) until the browser tab was reloaded. It now keeps retrying on a calm cadence, reconnects immediately when the browser reports the network is back, and reaps a connection attempt that hangs. (FR-01.28)
