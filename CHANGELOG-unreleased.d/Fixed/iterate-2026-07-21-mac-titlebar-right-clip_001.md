The title bar now reaches the right edge of the window on every screen. The app shell permanently reserved a scrollbar-wide strip (15px) on the right of the scroll area, and the title bars render inside it, so each bar stopped short and the photo backdrop showed through the gap — most visible on macOS Edge and Safari. Settings, the last screen still scrolling as a whole page, now scrolls its content below its title bar like every other screen, which is what makes the reserved strip unnecessary.
