Text that sits directly on the backdrop photo is readable again. The section labels on the Board (the CAMPAIGNS heading), on the Inbox (each project's group header and its "N open" count) and on the Triage list (the project names and the ANALYSIS / CODE-REVIEW / … group headers) were painted in dark ink that disappeared against the dark rigging in the photo and washed out against the bright sky. They now switch to light ink whenever they ride over the photo — and stay dark on any solid card — with no text-shadow. The Board's three columns (Backlog / In Progress / Done) now sit on their own light panels with a coloured top stripe, so the cards read on a clear surface instead of floating loose on the photo. And the status filter is now a single compact funnel button in the toolbar: it opens a small menu with an "All" row plus every state, each showing its count and a tick on the ones you have picked — replacing the old strip of status chips. It filters exactly as before; only the way you reach it has changed.
